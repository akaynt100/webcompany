Symptoms:
  • Bent operating rod
  • Lost operating rod
Cause:
The manual valve operating rod can become lost or damaged during a rebuild, making it necessary to rob cores for replacement parts.
Correction:
Sonnax has developed a direct replacement manual valve operating rod to prevent scrapping of expensive cores.
